About Health Monitor

Health Monitor is a simple, early detection tool that helps track your vital signs in real-time. You can connect wearables like Fitbit and Withings, or manually submit your own readings.

If unusual values are detected, you’ll see alerts instantly on your dashboard. Our goal: give you clarity about your health — instead of guessing or ignoring early signs.

✅ Track — temperature, heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar ✅ Connect — wearables for automatic sync ✅ Get Alerts — see warnings when something looks unusual

This is our MVP — we’d love your feedback to help shape the next version.
